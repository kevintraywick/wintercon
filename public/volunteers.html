How it works

  1. Sign up below — two minutes, no essay.
  2. We match you to shifts and confirm by text or email. Most shifts are 2–3 hours; about 4–6 hours across the weekend still leaves plenty of time to play.
  3. Check in at the volunteer desk 15 minutes before your shift. Breakfast first.
